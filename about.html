ABOUT THE TRAINER

Atish Shakergaye is a SEBI Registered Research Analyst (INH000006086) with over 20 years of hands-on experience in the Indian financial markets. As the founder of Market Credo, Bhopal's premier technical analysis training institute, Atish has spent two decades not just studying the markets — but living them, trading them, and distilling their deepest lessons into a curriculum that transforms how students see price.

With 20 years of real market cycles behind him — bull runs, bear crashes, consolidations, and breakouts — Atish brings something no textbook can replicate: the intuition and pattern recognition of someone who has been reading charts every single day for two decades. He has built this 20-year journey into a structured programme that teaches you the core concepts of technical analysis from absolute first principles.

"After 20 years in the markets, I have learned one truth — the charts always speak. My job is to teach you the language so you can hear what they are saying, in any market, at any time." — Atish Shakergaye · Founder, Market Credo · SEBI RA · INH000006086
TEACHING PHILOSOPHY

Atish does not hand out signals. He does not tell you what to buy or sell. Instead, he teaches you chart structure — how markets are architecturally built. He teaches you chart flow — the rhythm and breathing of price. He reveals the beauty hidden in charts — the visual logic that emerges once you truly understand price action. Practice makes a man perfect. Every class is built around real chart analysis, 20 years of market examples, and live practice.
