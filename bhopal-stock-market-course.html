What You Will Learn

  • Chart Structure: The foundational framework of how to read any chart — bar charts, candlestick charts, and line charts across all timeframes.
  • Chart Flow: Understanding how price moves, trends develop, and how to identify the direction of the market before placing a trade.
  • Chart Patterns: In-depth study of reversal and continuation patterns including Head & Shoulders, Double Top/Bottom, Triangles, Flags, Wedges, and more.
  • Candlestick Mastery: Complete candlestick analysis including single, dual, and triple candlestick patterns and their significance in real trading scenarios.
  • Support & Resistance: Identifying and drawing key levels where institutions buy and sell, and how to use these levels for precise entries and exits.
  • Price Action Trading: Reading raw price movement without indicators to understand market sentiment and make high-probability trading decisions.
  • Risk Management: Position sizing, stop-loss placement, risk-reward ratios, and capital preservation strategies that protect your trading account.
  • Trading Psychology: Understanding fear, greed, FOMO, and overtrading — and developing the mental discipline essential for long-term profitability.
  • Multi-Market Application: Applying technical analysis across equities (NSE, BSE), commodities (MCX), currencies (forex), and cryptocurrency markets.
